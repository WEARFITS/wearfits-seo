The Gen-AI Revolution in Fashion

Generative AI is not just a buzzword. In 2026, it's the backbone of personalized commerce. WearFits.me is at the forefront of this shift.

Generative AI (Gen-AI) allows retailers to create photorealistic images of garments on any body type, posture, and in any setting. This eliminates the need for expensive photoshoots for every possible size and body shape combination.

Key Technologies:

  • Diffusion Models: Used for creating realistic fabric textures and lighting.
  • Pose Estimation: Ensuring the garment fits the user's current posture perfectly.
  • Body Parsing: Distinguishing between the person and the garment for clean transitions.
